NIGHT LIFE IN TEL AVIV

Tel Aviv is the place to be if you want to enjoy some awesome nightlife! Without a doubt, Tel Aviv is the beating heart of Israel. We lived there for a few years, but once you step away for a little bit (we eventually headed for the Galilee hills with three littl’uns in tow), you realize just how fast the city is growing, how young and pulsing, never sleeping it truly is.
